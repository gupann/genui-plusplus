We've listed some frequently asked questions and their solutions below. If the problem you encountered is not on the list, you can tap Feedback to contact us.

Open the app and press Start to begin counting your steps. The counter works automatically in the background. Check your daily progress on the main dashboard.
If counting stops unexpectedly, ensure the app has permission to run in the background. Go to Settings → Apps → Battery and set it to "Unrestricted" or "Don't optimise".
The step counter uses an accelerometer algorithm to distinguish walking from random shaking. Adjust the sensitivity in Settings → Sensitivity if false counts occur frequently.
The app uses GPS and motion sensors together to detect driving vs walking. Enable "Auto-pause while in vehicle" in Settings to prevent false counts during car journeys.
Step count accuracy depends on phone placement and walking style. Carrying the phone in your pocket or hand gives the best results. Calibrate in Settings → Calibration for improved precision.
